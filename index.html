Our Story

SEVRA Travel was born from a story that began in January 2024, when Mic and Jordan met in London during a business trip. Jordan, based in London and working in real estate and property management, and Mic, based in New York and constantly traveling the world for work, began meeting across the globe—first in Paris, then Italy, and later Spain.

Spain quickly became a shared love, especially the Balearic Islands. In Spring 2025, during a return trip to Ibiza—their happy place—everything changed. While spending a day on a boat near the magnetic island of Es Vedrà, Jordan opened up about his feelings and his hopes for their future. Inspired by that moment, he made the decision to reshape his life around what truly mattered: their journey together.

Realizing that Mic’s world in luxury hospitality aligned with his own passions, Jordan chose to embark on a new path as a travel advisor under Little Emperor. Together, they searched for a name that honored the magical place where this new chapter began. SEVRA—born from “Es Vedrà”—became the perfect symbol of their story: a blend of magic, intention, love, and adventure.

Today, Jordan fully embraces this new professional chapter, combining his hospitality sensibility, global perspective, and deep appreciation for travel. SEVRA Travel represents not just luxury journeys—but the journey of two people building a beautiful future together.
